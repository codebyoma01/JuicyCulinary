Common Questions

Things people
often ask us

How far in advance should I order?

We recommend at least 5–7 days for standard cakes and a minimum of 3 weeks for wedding cakes. For very intricate designs or multi-tier orders, 4–6 weeks is ideal. If you're in a rush, message us on WhatsApp first and we'll tell you honestly what's possible.

Do you do cake tastings?

Yes, for wedding cakes we offer tasting sessions by appointment. A small fee applies which is credited toward your final order. Reach out via WhatsApp to schedule yours at least 2 weeks before you need to confirm your order.

How do I pay? What's the deposit?

We accept bank transfers to all major Nigerian banks. A 50% deposit is required to confirm and schedule your order. The remaining balance is due on or before delivery. We'll send you account details once your order is confirmed.

Can I send you an inspiration photo?

Absolutely and we encourage it. Send photos via WhatsApp or attach them to your order form. We'll use it as a reference while making your design entirely unique. We never copy another baker's work exactly.

Do you cater for dietary requirements?

We can accommodate some dietary needs just let us know in your order notes. Currently we offer eggless options for certain cake types. For severe allergies, please discuss with us directly before ordering so we can advise honestly.

How is the cake delivered safely?

All cakes are packed in sturdy boxes with non-slip bases and transported carefully in temperature-controlled conditions. For multi-tier wedding cakes, we deliver and set up at the venue personally. Delivery fees vary by location within Abuja FCT.
